Letter from the Secretary General


Greetings delegates!

As the Secretary-General, I welcome you to the RNPS MUN 2024: The 8th Edition.
Over the years, RNPS MUN has established itself as a platform where young minds with exceptional problem-solving skills can learn, grow, and shine. My journey is not just filled with research, debates and negotiations but also with a confidence that has helped me become who I am today.
I’m here today as your Secretary General because 8th-grade me, pulled out all the courage she had and registered for her very first MUN. This year’s intra-school MUN is the perfect place to be. It’s not just about the trophy or the title; it’s about the experience. In the years to come, it’s the skills you pick up in these two days that will do all the talking.Prepare to dive into fiery debates, embrace challenges and let the diplomat in you take over. I strongly believe that every one of you has it in you to climb the ladder and make the 8th Edition of this wonderful tradition a huge success.

I look forward to seeing you all make your mark this year and I wish you the best of luck!
What is RNPSMUN?


An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented.

Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will help sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!
